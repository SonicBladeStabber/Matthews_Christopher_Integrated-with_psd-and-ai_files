New with this DLC

Reaper

The Reaper is a melee DPS job, adorned in a menacing black cloak and able to summon a spectral avatar from the Void. The two-handed scythe serves as the Reaper’s main weapon to take down enemies at close-range, first seen as the primary weapon for the Dark Knight job in Final Fantasy XI.

Sage

The Sage is a healer class, and uses detachable Aether imbued Nouliths to aid teammates in battle, whether that’s healing, buffing, or protecting the party using barriers. Little is known about the unlock requirements, but like other classes you’ll need to be level 70 or higher in either Discipline of War or Magic.

Trials/Dungeons

New Trials and Dungeons is to be expected of a new DLC, one of which that we know of is the trial boss Anima. the summon from Final Fantacy 10 (FF-X). No additional information is available on other trials or dungeons.

Brand New Areas

There are more areas/maps to explore as you resume the story of the new DLC. Four areas are to be explored upon release, their size unknown for now.

Continued Story

The Story continues for the warrior of light and their party/companions. The Main story arc will end, and a new arc will begin in later patches after release. nothing else is known about the story.
