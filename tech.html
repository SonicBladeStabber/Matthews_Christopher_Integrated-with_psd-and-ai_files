Haptic Gloves are a form of vr accessory that restricts finger movement sorresponding to the object’s shape in the virtual world. It’s supposed to simulate realistic gripping for immersion.

Decagear

Decagear is the most advanced VR devices yet. It has functional facial tracking, precise room scale tracking, and controllers that have more interactive controlls making “A middle finger to enimies possible.”

Omni One

Omni-one is a movement type vr accessory wich allows to user to move in game using their legs by walking in place. You are still able to crouch and you can still jump if you wanted to, but going prone is no longer an option.
